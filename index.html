Landlords

Review Applicants, Approve, & Choose Your Lease

Applicants will be sent to you in real time. But don't worry about vetting them, Tenlor's got you covered. Review background checks, credit reports, renter's history and more. When a decision has been made, choose your lease template or upload a new one and send it away!

Tenants

Pay Rent, Manage Service Requests, Communicate with Management & More

Your home should be your sanctuary, a stress free place. Let Tenlor make it that way for you. Use your tenant dashboard to pay your rent each month, message your landlord to tell them of an issue or to just say 'Hey!'. Spend your time on the important things in life instead.
